Chocolate cake

ingredents

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • expresso powder
  • milk
  • oil
  • Egg
  • vanila extract
  • boiling water

Procedures

step 1 preheat the oven to 360f, prepare two 9-inch cake pans and butter lightly

step 2 mix the dry ingredents. adding the flour, coco powder, baking poweder, salt and expresso powder into a large bowl and mix well.

step 3 mix in the wet ingredents, ading the milk, oil, egges and vanila to the flourmixture and together. add boiling water to the batter untill well mixed

step 4 distribute the cake batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

step 5 Allow to cool for 10 minutes after its out from the oven and frost with chocolate butter cream frosting
